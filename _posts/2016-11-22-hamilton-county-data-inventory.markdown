One of the key challenges governments face in making data open is identifying which datasets to prioritize. 

We reviewed the records and search tools available on [Hamilton County's website](http://hamiltontn.gov) and compared them to the datasets and visualizations published on Baton Rouge's and Chattanooga's respective data portals.

Some Hamilton County records are already available in PDF format or through Web search forms. Those sets are identified in the tables below and would serve as a good starting point for implementing a data policy.
